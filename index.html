      Hand-hold guide — M50
      • Min safe shutter = 1 ÷ (focal length × 1.6 crop factor)
      • 15mm → min 1/25s · 22mm → min 1/35s · 45mm → min 1/75s
      • IS on the 15-45mm buys ~3 extra stops — usable to ~1/6s
      • 22mm has no IS — tuck elbows in, hold breath, shoot burst
